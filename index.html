The Mid-Market Challenge

Understanding the critical pain points of companies with 500-1,000 employees

01

The "Access Gap" to Top-Tier Expertise

Challenge:

Mid-sized firms struggle to access premium consulting expertise. They lack budget for Big 3 (MBB) firm overheads, yet boutique firms feel risky or lack specific niche capabilities.

Consultport Solution:

Democratized Access to Elite Talent. Consultport aggregates the best freelance management consultants, providing ex-MBB and top-tier expertise without massive agency premiums. This unlocks a talent pool previously reserved for large enterprises at accessible price points.

02

Inability to Scale Teams Quickly

Challenge:

When strategic projects or transformations arise, internal HR is too slow. Hiring specialized full-time talent takes months, causing missed opportunities.

Consultport Solution:

Speed and Platform Efficiency. Consultport's platform model enables booking "faster than ever before" - reducing sourcing friction from 6-8 weeks to days, allowing companies to scale project teams dynamically to match immediate demands.

03

The "Generalist" Internal Resource Trap

Challenge:

Lean internal teams at this size are forced to tackle complex specialized projects (digital transformation, M&A integration), leading to burnout and distraction from core operations.

Consultport Solution:

Flexible, Specialized Skill Injection. Consultport offers flexible consulting services - companies can inject specific subject-matter experts (e.g., Digital Transformation Lead) solely for project duration, without overburdening generalist managers.

04

Vetting and Trust Risk

Challenge:

Mid-sized firms lack sophisticated procurement to verify independent contractor quality, making freelance markets feel like a gamble with high stakes.

Consultport Solution:

Curated Quality Assurance. Consultport acts as the trust layer, handling vetting and evaluation. Positioning as "the best consultants" provides de-risked engagements through platform quality assurance.
